    AntTrails is simple and interactive application for visually displaying information from the boothing locations around campus. Reserve your spot, see a map view of occupied spots, edit your spot information at any time with a password and all information is cleared every 24 hours.This project utlized Google App Engine, Google Maps API to create and interactive tool for student vending at UC Irvine. AntTrails attempts to connect students to students to student vendors, increasing sales and forming a community.


Problem Statement & Design

    Each day dozens of groups and vendors take to the core of campus across the country to raise funds, awareness or garner new members. Most of these groups take to social media or rely on accidental encounters with passers by. AntTrails aimmed to create a tool that would act as a daily directory of commonly used boothing spaces.


Initial White Board Concept

    Our team used Google Maps and matched each available booth location to a geographic coordinance and a booth number, then created a simple form that linked each spot to a sign up.
