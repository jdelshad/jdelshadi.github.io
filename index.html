A Quick Biography & Welcome

Hi there! My name is John, I am a young professional whose eager to fill more than a few hats. I specialize in Project Management, Human Computer Interaction and striving to learn more about Web and Product design.

I graduated with a degree in Human Computer Interaction from the University of California, Irvine. There I had the opportunity to collaborate on projects with the UC Irvine School of Medicine, The Aerospace Corporation and others.

Since then, I was part of a company wide software and training project at the Irvine Company. Now I am in Program Management at Panasonic Avionics Corporation, working with global airlines and thier partners to deliver best in class in-flight entertainment systems.
