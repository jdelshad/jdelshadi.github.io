Designing for New Student Orientation
July, 7th 2015 Client: New Student Orientation, UC Irvine

I was asked by staff at UC Irvine's New Student Orientation to design a cover for their Tranfer Success Program, we went through a few iteration of the design but the final product turned out better than I expected. The theme of this years is "Evergreen" and the includes a quote from Desmond Tutu. Which I had orginally left out in the first iteration. I am generally not a fan of block quotes, but breaking it up seems to work here.
